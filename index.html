About Us

The Faiyaz family is a close-knit household comprising seven members, centered around Safdur Faiyaz and his wife Aabda Khatoon. Their son Kamaluddin is married to Bushra Naheed, and together they have three children: Ayan, Ishan, and Amaira. Completing the family circle are the maternal grandparents, Qamru Zaman and Gulshan Naaz, who play a cherished role in the lives of their grandchildren. This multi-generational family represents a rich tapestry of relationships, love, and shared experiences, with each member contributing to the warmth and dynamics of their household.
